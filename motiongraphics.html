Motion Graphics

Motion graphics refer to the use of animation, visual effects, and graphic design techniques to create moving images or animations. It combines elements such as text, illustrations, shapes, icons, and photographs with movement, transitions, and timing to convey information, tell stories, or evoke emotions.
